PEERS Dataset

The Penn Electrophysiology of Encoding and Retrieval Study (PEERS) is a multi-session experiment looking at scalp EEG during free recall and recognition. It recruits both younger adults (16-30) and older adults (60-90). For now, we exclusively use the free recall trials performed by younger adult participants.
